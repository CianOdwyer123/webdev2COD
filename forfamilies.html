In Dunboyne we have a few leisure spots. The main one of these would be the Dunboyne village public park. This would be a great walk to bring your dog on so it can have a nice sniff. Also within this park there is a playground with many play areas for the younger kids. While the little kids are occupied in the playground, the older kids to teenagers can play at the skate park. The skatepark has 7 ramps and a bike rack.

  • Get The Train into Dublin City:

    • In Dunboyne we are blessed with a train station that will bring you anywhere in the country.

  • Get The Bus To Blanchardstown Shopping Centre or Dublin city:

    • Another form of transport is Dublin bus. This bus will bring you from Dunboyne all around Blanchardstown and Dublin City.

  • Community Centre:

    1. A gym.
    2. A Basketball court.
    3. A Badminton court.

  • GAA pitches:

    • In Dunboyne we have 2 sets of GAA pitches. Although these pitches are used for matches and training, when they are not in use, the public can use them to puck a ball around. These pitches are specifically designed for the sports:

    1. Hurling.
    2. Gaelic football.
